I'm a Software Engineer

Over the eight years that I have worked in education, whether as a paraeducator directly assisting special needs students in the classroom, or as a pre-algebra student teacher implementing a UBD unit plan, or in the high school geometry class organizining group work using the jigsaw strategy, creative design has always been an undercurrent of my work as a teacher. My job was to combine research-based best practices and then create and orchestrate student experiences to maximize understanding. As a software engineer, I continue to solve new and intriguing problems to create tools and experiences that enrich the lives of others with enduring value.
